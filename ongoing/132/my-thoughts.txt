The video is a treasure trove of actionable advice for career growth, from a seasoned professional, Ethan Evans, whose success attests to his insights. As summarised in my notes:

1. Effectively discharge your existing roles: This is critical before considering further growth.
2. Proactively seek to assist your manager: This activity undoubtedly improves rapport and indicates your readiness to support beyond your assigned tasks.
3. Carry out tasks assigned by your manager diligently, even the less exciting ones. They build trust.
4. Engage in continuous professional dialogue with your manager: Share your aspirational roles and skills, and seek to align tasks to these goals.
5. Keep repeating these steps: Consistent engagement with your manager fosters a supportive relationship and helps you move towards your career objectives.
